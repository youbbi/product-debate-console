üìè Consensus Measurement

Each executive provides a recommendation. The system calculates how closely they agree:

High Alignment (85%) Team agrees

Moderate (60%) Some debate

Low (35%) Team divided
>75% ‚Äî Strong consensus, confident decision
50-75% ‚Äî Moderate agreement, review tradeoffs
<50% ‚Äî Team divided, needs discussion

üéØ Decision Output

GO
Execute the decision with confidence. Team aligned, benefits clear.
PIVOT
Proceed with modifications based on executive feedback.
HOLD
Defer decision. More analysis or alignment needed.

Each decision includes: confidence level, executive summary, consensus points, disagreements, and next steps.
